About Us

Education is powerful— it’s the key to development! We’re a group of UC Berkeley students from diverse majors and backgrounds seeking to learn about computer science education systems in developing countries and how we can help improve them. In the past, we have worked with She Can Code Rwanda, a nonprofit started and run by Rwandans to teach girls how to code.

What We Do

We partner with organizations on specific projects related to CS education in developing nations. Currently, we are in the process of working with organizations to launch CS education-related projects in schools across South Asia.

If you’re interested in contributing to tangible projects impacting students’ lives, please contact us. Current presidents and points of contact: Jie Qiu (jieq@berkeley.edu) and Kartiki Gaur (kartiki.gaur@berkeley.edu).
